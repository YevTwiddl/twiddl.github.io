About product:

Wisp simplifies data management by connecting all your data—across multiple computers and formats—into a single, queryable database. No more moving raw files or dealing with complex setups. Whether it’s stored in XLS, CSV, JSON, or Parquet, your data remains in place, but becomes instantly accessible.

With just one click, Wisp brings all your scattered data into one central hub, ready to be used without needing any technical expertise. It’s a fast, simple way to unlock the value of your distributed data without ever leaving your original source.


Current Status: PoC in Progress

Wisp is currently in the Proof of Concept (PoC) phase. This stage is all about demonstrating the core concept and building the foundation for Wisp’s full potential. After the PoC, the next steps involve refining the product for usability, scalability, and adding advanced features based on real-world feedback.
